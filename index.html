Words About Us

We are a Professional Organization with dedicated team of qualified Insurance Professionals, Risk Engineers, Chartered Accountants and specialized Management Graduates to service various Insurance and Risk Management requirements of clients spread across the country. We are located in cities across India, specialized in evolving Insurance Portfolio Management Solutions for the corporate sector across the spectrum of industries with above 200 satisfied Clients.

Services

From analyzing and covering exposures, to loss control and managing claims, our comprehensive approach offers guidance, protection and peace of mind to our corporate clients in today's copious business models. Our approach to Insurance is holistic. We critically view the need and adequacy of Insurance from a client's perspective. When considering an insurance company, we evaluate the quality and level of service they provide, in addition to a competitive pricing structure

We have established a service team comprising insurance professionals with relevant industry expertise and hands-on experience on direct insurance requirements. Our service team has undergone comprehensive training (and has the mandatory regulatory approval) to provide professional services to clients. Our focus is to fulfill the risk and insurance needs of large & mid-sized corporate and public entities.

The Business philosophy of Securisk is designed to a customer oriented & partnership approach with a lot of innovation and customization. We stay focused on one goal: Help clients and be part of the success.
